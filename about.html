About me:

There are many words that I could describe myself, but I'll try to be sweet and simple. I originally come from Lithuania and have lived in Denmark over 5 years now. Throughout this span of time I've worked many jobs. I've stacked boxes, delivered food, made food, dealt with customers as airport security. Name it, I've done it. And now I've decided to learn a new and challenging skill - web development. However, computer languages are not only languages I can speak. I also speak 4 languages and Danish is one of them. I am not perfect yet, but I do manage to communicate to a certain extent. As a person I'm communicative, curious, self-motivated and both solo & team player. In the future I'd like to develop my design, coding and critical thinking skills.
