Hvem er jeg?

I come from the Baltic States, in particular - Lithuania and have lived in Denmark for over 6 years now. I've worked many jobs in the years. I've stacked boxes, delivered and made food, provided customer service. Finally, being avidly interested in computers I've decided to learn a new and challenging skill - web development. However, computer languages are not only languages I can speak. I speak 4 languages in total, Danish included. I am not perfect yet, but I do manage to communicate to a certain extent and I've even completed a Danish language course at B2 level (I still struggle with 'Rødgrød med fløde' though!). As a person I'm communicative, inquisitive, patient, self-motivated and am flexible enough to become both a solo or a team player. In the future I'd like to become a full-stack developer.
